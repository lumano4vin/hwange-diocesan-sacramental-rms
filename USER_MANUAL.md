# Hwange Diocesan Sacramental Database System - Unified User Manual (v3.0)

Welcome to the official help documentation for the Catholic Diocese of Hwange - Zimbabwe. This guide covers all functional steps for accessing and using the system to manage canonical records.

---

## 1. Accessing the System

Before you can sign in, ensure the system is running by following the steps in the `PREREQUISITES.md` file (usually double-clicking `LAUNCH_RMS.bat`).

### How to Sign In
1.  Open your web browser and navigate to `http://localhost:8000`.
2.  On the **Login** page:
    *   **Username**: Enter your assigned username (e.g., your surname, parish identifier, or `bishop_hwange` for the Bishop).
    *   **Password**: Enter your secure password (default temporary password: `Hwange2026!`).
3.  Click **Login**.

> [!NOTE]
> **Episcopal Access**: The Bishop of Hwange (**Rt. Rev. Raphael Mabuza Ncube**) is greeted dynamically by name upon logging in and signing out, accessing the system under the dedicated username `bishop_hwange`.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If it is your first time logging in, the system will prompt you to change your temporary password for security reasons.

---

## 2. Navigation & Interface

The system uses a **Unified Dashboard** with a premium design language.

*   **Universal Search**: Use the search bar at the top (or `Alt+S`) to find any parishioner instantly.
*   **Oversight Cards**: Quick metrics at the top of the dashboard showing active faithful and recent sacraments.
*   **Sidebar**: Your main menu for Parishes, Clergy, Sacraments, and the Communication Hub.

---

## 3. The Canonical Handshake (Handover Protocol)

When a priest is reassigned, a digital handover process is initiated to ensure registry integrity:

1.  **Outgoing Sign-Off**: The leaving priest must log into the **Handover Hub** and provide a status report on the registry.
2.  **Inventory Check**: Physical registries must be verified against digital counts in the system.
3.  **Succession Acceptance**: The new priest reviews the notes and "Accepts" the mission, transferring access rights.

---

## 4. Communication Hub

The **Communication Hub** is the official digital channel between your Mission and the Diocesan Administration.

*   **Tickets**: Every request is tracked for transparency.
*   **Categories**: Choose from Registry Correction, Dispensation Request, or General Query.
*   **Notifications**: Look for the bell icon for responses from the Chancery.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting & Support

*   **Forgotten Password**: Click "Forgot password?" on the login page to initiate a reset via email.
*   **Account Locked**: After 5 failed attempts, your account is locked for 15 minutes.
*   **Invalid Credentials**: Ensure Caps Lock is off and verify your username with the Information Office.

---

## Support
For urgent canonical or technical assistance, contact the **Diocesan Information Office** or your local Parish System Administrator.
