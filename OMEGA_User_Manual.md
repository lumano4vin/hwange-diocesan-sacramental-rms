## Diocese of Hwange Sacramental RMS • OMEGA Edition v39.0

### 1. Introduction
The **Annua Statistica OMEGA (v39.0)** system is a high-fidelity reporting engine designed to fulfill the canonical requirements for "Total Accountability" across all parish missions. Starting with v39.0, the system implements **Total Itemization**, ensuring that every statistical metric—from guild members to medical staff—is displayed as a separate, transparent line item for audit purposes.

### 2. Accessing the Report
1. Log in to the **Diocesan Sacramental RMS**.
2. From the main dashboard, navigate to **Parish Reports**.
3. Select the target **Parish/Mission** and the **Reporting Year**.
4. Click on the **OMEGA Edition** tab to open the advanced reporting interface.

### 3. Data Entry Modules
The report is divided into ten (10) mandatory sections:

*   **I. Churches and Pastoral Centres**: Track Blessed Sacrament locations and pastoral engagement (Male/Female attendance).
*   **II. Governance and Personnel**: Record the ecclesiastical leadership status and a detailed census of Priests, Deacons, Religious, and Catechists.
*   **III. Vocations Pipeline**: Monitor the future of the diocese, including major seminarians (Philosophy vs. Theology) and religious training stages (Novices, Postulants, etc.).
*   **IV. Parish Guilds & Sacraments**: Track member counts for major associations (Legion of Mary, CYA, etc.) and key sacramental milestones.
*   **V. Education Matrix**: A comprehensive census of students and teachers across all school levels (Pre-school to Commercial), separated by faith and gender.
*   **VI. Health & Care Matrix**: Detailed reporting on Mission Hospitals, Clinics (including Baby Clinics), Patient counts, Mortality, and specialized care (Elderly, Disabled, Orphans). Medical staff are now itemized by role and ecclesiastical status.
*   **VII. Financial Ledger**: A granular 14-line ledger for all diocesan collections. **Supports multi-currency reporting (USD, ZiG, ZAR, BWP).**
*   **VIII. Status Animarum**: Population dynamics tracking the balance of Catholics through Baptisms, Immigrants (Transferred In), Deaths, and Emigrants (Transferred Out).
*   **IX. Marriage Accountability**: Canonical breakdown of marriages (Between Catholics, Mixed Religion, Disparity of Cult).
*   **X. Observations**: Qualitative pastoral reporting for additional context or challenges.

### 4. Smart Automation Features
*   **Auto-Fetch**: The system automatically pulls totals for **Baptisms, Deaths, and Marriages** directly from the digital registry tables. These fields appear as pre-filled values to ensure data synchronization.
*   **Dynamic Balancing**: The *Status Animarum* section automatically calculates the "Balance" between year-start and year-end based on the entries provided.
*   **Multi-Currency Logic**: Selecting a currency automatically prepends the correct symbol (e.g., USD, ZiG) to all financial lines and totals.

### 5. Exporting for the Archive
To generate the final document for canonical submission:
1. Ensure all sections are saved using the **"Save OMEGA Report Data"** button.
2. Review the **"Total Report Preview"** at the bottom of the page.
3. Click **"Export Final Canonical PDF"**.
4. The system will generate a multi-page document featuring:
    *   **Official Diocese Seal** in the header.
    *   **Automated Pagination** (Page X of Y).
    *   **Bilingual Titles** (English and Latin).
    *   **Archival Signature Lines** for the Parish Priest and Diocesan Chancellor.

### 6. Archival Best Practices
*   **Frequency**: Reports should be finalized and exported annually by December 31st.
*   **Verification**: Once exported, the PDF should be printed, signed, and stamped by the Parish Priest before being submitted to the Chancery for the Chancellor's signature.
*   **Integrity**: Ensure the "Observations" section is used to explain any significant fluctuations in statistics (e.g., massive immigration or school closures).
